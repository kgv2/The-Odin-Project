Shoyu Ramen

Description

Shoyu ramen is a Japanese noodle soup with a soy sauce-based broth, offering a savory and slightly tangy flavor.
The broth is often made with chicken or pork stock and flavored with garlic, ginger, and green onions.

Served with thin noodles, it's topped with ingredients like chashu pork, marinated egg, bamboo shoots, and nori.
Shoyu ramen is a comforting and flavorful dish enjoyed worldwide.
